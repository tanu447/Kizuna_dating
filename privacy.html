Privacy & Policy

Kizuna Privacy Policy is designed to protect and respect the privacy of its users, ensuring that personal information is collected, stored, and used in a secure and transparent manner. When users register on the platform, Kizuna Dating collects essential personal details, such as name, email address, date of birth, and location, to facilitate the matchmaking process and enhance user experience. This information is stored securely and used solely for the purpose of providing services, improving the platform, and facilitating communication between users. Kizuna Dating is committed to safeguarding user data in compliance with relevant data protection laws and regulations. Personal information is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes without explicit consent from the user. The platform also employs advanced encryption methods to protect sensitive data during transmission and storage. However, users are advised to take personal responsibility for maintaining the confidentiality of their own login credentials and should exercise caution when sharing any personal information with others on the platform. Kizuna Dating also collects non-personal information such as device type, IP address, and browsing activity, which is used to improve the functionality of the platform, troubleshoot issues, and better understand user preferences. Users can review, update, or delete their personal information at any time by accessing their account settings. In case of any data breach or security incident, Kizuna Dating will notify users promptly and take the necessary steps to minimize any potential harm. By using the platform, users consent to the collection and use of their data in accordance with this Privacy Policy, and they acknowledge that it may be updated periodically. Regular review of this policy is recommended to ensure that users are aware of any changes that may affect their privacy.
  • 1. Personal Information Collection: Kizuna Dating collects essential personal details such as name, email, date of birth, and location during registration to provide matchmaking services and enhance user experience.
  • 2. Data Usage: The information collected is used solely to provide services, improve platform functionality, and facilitate communication between users. It is not shared for marketing purposes without user consent.
  • 3. Data Security: Kizuna Dating employs advanced encryption techniques to protect users' personal data from unauthorized access, both during transmission and storage.
  • 4. Non-Personal Information: The platform collects non-personal data, including device type, IP address, and browsing activity, to improve site functionality, troubleshoot, and personalize the user experience.
  • 5. User Responsibility: Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials and should exercise caution when sharing personal information with others on the platform.
  • 6. Data Retention and Access: Users can review, update, or delete their personal information at any time through their account settings.
  • 7. No Sale of Information: Kizuna Dating does not sell or share users' personal information with third parties for marketing purposes without explicit consent.
  • 8. Third-Party Sharing: User information may be shared with trusted third-party service providers only to facilitate the services provided by Kizuna Dating, but these parties are obligated to maintain confidentiality.
  • 9. Security Breaches: In the event of a data breach or security incident, Kizuna Dating will notify users promptly and take steps to minimize potential harm.
  • 10. Policy Updates: The Privacy Policy may be updated periodically, and users are encouraged to review it regularly to stay informed about any changes.
  • 11. Consent: By using the platform, users consent to the collection and use of their personal data as outlined in this Privacy Policy.
